The simulation is defined by days

[day, patientID , assignedTo, ventilatorBed , estimatedLOS, discharge , death ]

day- {1,2,...}
patientID-ID OF Patient
assignedTo- Identifier of reception bed, or Null if patient is not assigned
ventilatorBed- 1 if it is ventilator bed, 0 otherwise. Null if patient is not assigned
estimatedLOS - integer representing the length of stay estimated for patient ID
discharge - 0 if it remains at the bed, 1 if it is discharged of the bed. Note that patient can be death, sane, or migrate to other bed
death    - 0 if not is death, 1 if it is death
